# Platform Adaptation

Only adapt after `imitation-draft.md` passes the imitation repair gate.

Before adapting, read `platform-patterns.md`. Do not rely on generic memory of "公众号" or "小红书". If the current topic lacks platform examples or the sources are only operator summaries, say so in the draft notes.

## WeChat

Goal: sustained reading, professional trust, and a complete reasoning chain.

Keep:

- the strongest opening scene;
- complete reasoning chain;
- restrained judgment;
- enough evidence, rewritten as prose;
- an ending that returns to the original human problem.

Avoid:

- naked links in body;
- footnote-looking paragraphs;
- generic AI slogans;
- feature-list structure;
- report language.

Suggested structure:

1. Title with clear issue and reader promise, not vague literary title only.
2. First screen: scene plus conflict/question.
3. Body: 2-4 section blocks with small headings when helpful.
4. Each block: one question, one story/evidence move, one clarified judgment.
5. Visual/format anchors for mobile reading: short paragraphs, occasional bold anchor or image suggestion.
6. Ending: return to human scene and leave a restrained reflection or discussion point.

## Xiaohongshu

Goal: visual click, fast comprehension, save/comment value, and scenario-led usefulness.

Keep:

- one concrete pain scene;
- one sharp question;
- one core insight;
- visual sequence suggestions;
- restrained discussion prompt.

Avoid:

- "姐妹们快冲";
- "不看后悔";
- overclaiming;
- medical/legal advice;
- private-message bait;
- stuffing policy citations into the post body.

Suggested carousel:

1. Cover: one concrete question or contrast, readable at thumbnail size.
2. Card 1: scene hook.
3. Card 2: why the intuitive explanation is incomplete.
4. Card 3: what can be confirmed.
5. Card 4: what the reader should look at next.
6. Card 5: one sentence insight.
7. Caption: short body, no citation scaffolding, one restrained discussion question.

Xiaohongshu body is not a shorter WeChat essay. It should be a card script plus caption. If a long article is simply line-broken into short paragraphs, the adaptation has failed.
